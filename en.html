Drag & drop to generate subtitles

WinWhisper is an offline transcription tool designed for Windows users. It converts audio / video files into plain text and subtitle files (SRT / VTT), ideal for podcasts, interviews, lectures and meeting notes.

Fully offline · No upload Whisper models built-in · Ready to use Export TXT / SRT / VTT
Download WinWhisper for Windows
Windows 10 / 11 x64 only. All processing is done locally on your machine.
Get to know WinWhisper in 1 minute
  • Drag audio / video files onto the main window to create a task Drag & drop
  • Automatically uses bundled ffmpeg to handle most media formats
  • High-quality speech recognition powered by Whisper
  • Generate TXT / SRT / VTT for editing, captioning and archiving
  • Everything stays on your device – perfect for sensitive recordings

Key features

Local & private
Transcription runs entirely on your computer, without uploading audio to any server. Suitable for internal meetings, interviews and classes where privacy matters.
Frictionless workflow
Drag audio / video files to the window to start. Tasks are created automatically and results are displayed in real time on the right.
Subtitles & history
One-click export to TXT / SRT / VTT for NLE tools. Built-in history panel lets you quickly reopen and reuse previous transcripts.
